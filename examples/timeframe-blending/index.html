Two-Timeframe Blending

Blend between 1-minute and 5-minute candles using mousewheel

About This Example

This example demonstrates blending between two different timeframes:

  • Two series with different candle durations (1m and 5m)
  • Mousewheel controls opacity blending between series
  • Smooth transition shows granular vs aggregated views
  • Demonstrates multi-series rendering capabilities

Try: Use the mousewheel while hovering over the chart to blend between timeframes
